About Me

I'm Chahat Kansal — a writer, strategist, and finance aspirant based in Ghaziabad, India. My background spans content creation, social media management, nonprofit fundraising, and team leadership, all shaped by a deep curiosity about how words move people and how numbers move markets.

I interned at Pledge A Smile Foundation, where I led donor engagement and crowdfunding campaigns, and at ArjaVA, where I progressed from intern to a managerial role within my tenure.
